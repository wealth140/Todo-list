📖 How to Use Todo Pro

✏️ Adding a Task

1. Type your task name in the text field

2. Select a due date using the date picker

3. Choose a priority level (Low, Medium, High)

4. Click "Add" or press Enter

🎯 Priority Levels

🔴 High Priority: Urgent tasks, do first

🟡 Medium Priority: Important, do soon

🟢 Low Priority: Can wait, do later

Tasks are automatically sorted by priority!

🗑️ Deleting a Task

Click the "Delete" button on any task to remove it

💾 Auto-Save

Your tasks are automatically saved to your device

Your data persists even after closing the app!
